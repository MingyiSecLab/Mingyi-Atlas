# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and tradeoffs rather than people.
- Assume good intent while still asking for clarity when needed.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive information.
- Follow the project's security boundaries, especially around pentest tooling.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Submitting or requesting unauthorized exploitation, credential theft, malware behavior, denial-of-service logic, or other harmful activity.
- Ignoring maintainer requests to remove sensitive data or unsafe content.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, restrict participation, or take other action needed to protect the project and its community.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers privately when possible. Do not include sensitive security details in a public issue.
